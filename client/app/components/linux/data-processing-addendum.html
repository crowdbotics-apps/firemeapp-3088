Last updated: December 26, 2019

We make it easy for Atlassian customers to sign and submit our Data Processing Addendum (DPA). The DPA helps meet onward transfer requirements under the GDPR. The DPA is pre-signed by Atlassian. If you have any questions about the DPA, please see our FAQ. Click the link below to download the DPA and email a signed copy to dpasubmission@atlassian.com.
